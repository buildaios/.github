# BuildAIOS Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of BuildAIOS, pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free and welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We commit to acting and interacting in ways that contribute to an open, friendly, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Expected Behaviour

Our community values technical excellence, precise execution, and architectural safety above Fedora Linux. We expect participants to demonstrate:

* Using welcoming and inclusive language.
* Being respectful of differing viewpoints and architectural constraints.
* Gracefully accepting constructive technical critique.
* Focusing on what is best for the overall safety, stability, and open-source integrations of the platform.
* Showing empathy toward other community members, developers, and users.

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

Harassment and other exclusionary behaviours are not tolerated in any part of the BuildAIOS space. Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:

* The use of sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
* Public or private harassment.
* Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without explicit permission.
* Conducting or promoting actions that compromise the safety of users' local environments or system infrastructure.
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional open-source environment.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces managed by BuildAIOS (including repositories, issue trackers, wiki sites, discussions, and associated communication bridges), and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.

---

## Reporting and Contact

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported by contacting the project safety team at:

**security@buildaios.dev**

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All community organizers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Community organizers will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct:

### 1. Correction
* **Community Impact:** Use of inappropriate language or other behaviour deemed unprofessional or unwelcome in the community.
* **Consequence:** A private, written warning from project maintainers, providing clarity on the nature of the violation and an explanation of why the behaviour was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning
* **Community Impact:** A violation through a single incident or ongoing pattern of inappropriate behaviour.
* **Consequence:** A warning with consequences for continued behaviour. No interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct, for a specified period of time. This includes avoiding interactions on project communication channels as well as external platforms. Violating these terms may lead to a temporary or permanent ban.

### 3. Temporary Ban
* **Community Impact:** A serious violation of community standards, including sustained harassment.
* **Consequence:** A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the project for a specified period of time. No public or private interaction with the project, its channels, or maintainers is allowed during this period.

### 4. Permanent Ban
* **Community Impact:** Demonstrating a pattern of violation, or a single egregious violation of community standards, including targeted harassment or malicious exploitation of environment privileges.
* **Consequence:** A permanent ban from any kind of public interaction within the BuildAIOS open-source community, repositories, and communication channels.
